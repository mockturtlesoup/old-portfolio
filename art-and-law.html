Art & Law Conference

Call for Papers

Code Design Logo

Typeface: Times / Helvetica

When we first juggled ideas for a new conference, I was passed the task of making it look like the sort of event every academic would like to speak at. The logo was a result of consensus between my leaning towards flat, geometric shapes, and my colleague’s fancy for Times (which was supposed to be fitting as a standard legal typeface). The team liked the parallax effect and the call for papers was sent out in the website form. The logo followed the published papers into a journal, and will accompany the second edition of the event.
