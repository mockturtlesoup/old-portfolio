ByeBuy

CMS from the ground up

Code Layout CMS

Typeface: Museo Sans / Titillium

The main purpose of my internship at ByeBuy was to implement a Content Management System for the company's non-commerce sites, design and write the pages and instruct the team on how to use and maintain them. After a few days of research, we agreed to use Kirby, a file CMS with no database, little overhead and less PHP compared to other solutions. Over the next few weeks, I worked towards the realease of new sites, starting with server configuration (Apache 2.4 Redhat Linux) and security, through designing the layouts to writing and debugging the front-end code. With the guidance of the CTO, I learned the basics of Linux system administration and together we improved The blog, jobs, help and legal pages are currently in use by the company.
