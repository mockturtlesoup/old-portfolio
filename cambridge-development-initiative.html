'Not Too Orange'

Code Design Identity

Typeface: Lato / EB Garamond

CDI needed to brush off their visual identity together with the website. The new color schema kept the warm tones but did away with the muddy shades, giving way to a more legible site. Trying to balance the large amount of text, I paired a chunky serif with a slim sans, which makes the plentiful paragraphs a little easier on the eye.
